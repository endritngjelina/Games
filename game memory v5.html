Welcome to Fruit Memory Match Deluxe!

This game will test your memory skills. Your goal is to find matching pairs of fruits.

Flip two cards at a time to find matches. The fewer moves you make, the higher your score!

Scoring System

Each match gives you points based on:

  • Base points: 100 per match
  • Time bonus: Faster matches = more points
  • Streak multiplier: Consecutive matches increase your points

Advanced Features

Use these tools to improve your game:

  • Hint: Reveal a matching pair (costs 50 points)
  • Daily Challenges: Complete for bonus rewards
  • Streaks: Consecutive matches multiply your points
